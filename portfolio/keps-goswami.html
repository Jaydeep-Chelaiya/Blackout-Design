- About Project

"Raw" is a sushi bar located in Kuwait. It is inspired on the traditional Japanese cuisine and their high quality standards. Our branding proposal keeps some details of this classical culture, represented by the vintage imagery.

The black brushstrokes that invade some of the collateral design represents the owner, a young Kuwaiti that is also the main Chef. His education and talent allows him to offer an authentic product while he imposes his particular style on every recipe.
